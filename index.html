How To Use

click anywhere on the screen to add a point.

To add an image, click on one the sample images in Your Image, or use the 'image URL' input to add the URL of an image. Then click ADD IMAGE, or press enter. This will add the image to 'Your Images' Then select on to add it to the background.

To edit a point, Set Mode to 'edit', and click and drag points click and drag the point. Alternatively, hold down CTRL while in any mode, then click and drag points.

To delete a point, Set Mode to 'delete', or hold down X, then click anywhere, and the nearest point will be deleted.

To Draw on the canvas, set mode to 'draw mode', or hold down D. Set the color you want to draw with, and click and drag to draw.

To erase your drawing, Set Mode to 'Erase drawing mode', or hold down SHIFT. Then click and drag to eraase.

To erase all drawing and points that have been added, click RESET CANVAS. Note that this will not reset the drawing color or the brush and eraser sizes.

Click the Settings Gear in the top right, to show or hide settings menu.
